Tessify.org

Healthy ecosystems by using microservices

Tessify.org is an organization that enables healthy ecosystems to work in. In a healthy ecosystem, people can grow and flourish by taking on challenges and developing new skills. The ecosystem can be a company, a government organization, a club, a school, a charity organization or it can even be an open system.

Together with its volunteers, Tessify.org creates a unique and robust open-source platform that facilitates the use of social microservices within the chosen ecosystem. It does this by connecting supply and demand. Work packages with the necessary micro training are provided by the client if needed. Anyone within the chosen ecosystem, which can be small or as big as the world, can respond to the demand. In this way, the platform enables people to grow and contribute to an ecosystem.
